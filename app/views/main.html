We are a parish in the East of England Deanery of the Diocese of Sourozh - the diocese of the Russian Orthodox Church which covers the British Isles. Our parish was founded in 1973 by Metropolitan Anthony of Sourozh. We have services every Saturday and Sunday. The services are primarily in English, but you will also hear Slavonic, Romanian and Greek.
