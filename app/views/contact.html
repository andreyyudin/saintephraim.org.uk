The spiritual leader of our parish is Father Raphael, who is the last priest to be ordained by the Archbishop Antony of Sourozh. Fr Raphael, who hails from Bedfordshire, and his American-born wife Carolyn are themselves converts to Orthodoxy. ‘Carolyn and I were received into the Orthodox Church in New York, almost 30 years ago, when I was working in Manhattan.’ he explains while declining to be drawn as to why British people might find Orthodoxy an attractive proposition.

Assistant Priest



Fr Evgeny Selensky


Father Evgeny (Russian Orthodox Church, Moscow Patriarchate, 2010) is our second priest who is kindly visiting us every second and fourth week of the month to serve to the needs of those parish members who would like to receive services in the Russian language.
In the past, he has done post-doctorate research in applied mathematics at Glasgow University, University College Cork and Keldysh Applied Mathematics Institute, Russian Academy of Science.
Fr Evgeny holds a PhD in Physics and Mathematics from the Faculty of Mechanics and Mathematics of Moscow State University, 1999. MSc Civil Engineering with distinction (Faculty of Robotics of Moscow State Technical University, 1995), Smolensk theological seminary with distinction, 2009.
At present, Father Evgeny works for a private company in logistics. His professional interests include logistics, routing and scheduling. Non-professionally, he is interested in the scientific and philosophical aspects of modern origin of life research (please see http://orthodoxchristian-blogger.blogspot.com for his personal bilingual blog). He is married and has four children.
